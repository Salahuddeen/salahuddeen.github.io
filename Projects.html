HackAgainstHunger Hackathon

Our solution was a web application that provided users with a means to donate goods and services to community gardens as well as assist in managing community gardens. The application was built to make use of firebase as a backend and the google maps api to locate farms. It used data from the open dataset that was provided to generate a heatmap of areas that were in need of community farms based on their hunger index.
